A student-run aeronautical design society that provides its members with the hands-on experience needed to connect the coursework knowledge with its real life application.

ABOUT US



Aero McGill was imagined by a group of undergraduate students at McGill with a shared passion for all things aerospace, to give like-minded students the opportunity to get their hands dirty with various aerospace projects, in a collaborative environment. We were inspired to start the team because of the lack of any aerospace-related design team at our school. McGill offers an undergraduate concentration in Aeronautical Engineering and a Masters program in Aerospace Engineering, and our faculty conducts ground-breaking research in collaboration with the vast Montreal aerospace industry. There are over 40 faculty members at McGill who concentrate on aerospace-related research, with over $20M per year in aerospace research programs, and over 4,500 students in the Faculty of Engineering at McGill. We saw an opportunity to give our fellow students an opportunity to learn more about aircraft design and apply the topics covered in classes like fluid mechanics, computational aerodynamics, and aircraft performance, stability, and control.
