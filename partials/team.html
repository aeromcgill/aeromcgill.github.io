Team

At Aero, we intend to provide our members, whether graduate or undergraduate students, with a real-life engineering challenge. The aim is to foster intellectual and interpersonal skills. Members will devote their time designing, building and testing aircrafts of different kinds and dimensions.

Our team currently consist of twenty-four engineering students from different engineering disciplines; mechanical, electrical, materials, and software. And we are currently in the recruitment for the year 2015-2016.
